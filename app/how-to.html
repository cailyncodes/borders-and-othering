How to use

This website can be used either with built-in guided tours or explored by oneself. If you are new to this site, you are strongly encouraged to start with the guided tour. You can switch between them at any time. Simply click or tap the buttons below.



Participants should keep an open mind and take steps towards recognizing inequities in the current narrative of maps and space.



Teachers and educators are encouraged to visit the Resources page for teaching guides, discussion questions, and commentary.
